Registration Process

  1. Complete the Form: Fill out the registration form with student and parent details.
  2. Submit Documents: Provide required documents (see below).
  3. Pay Registration Fee: Submit the fee via the Payments page.
  4. Confirmation: Receive a confirmation email within 5-7 days.

Requirements

  • Birth Certificate (copy)
  • Previous School Records/Transcripts
  • Parent/Guardian ID (copy)
  • Proof of Address (e.g., utility bill)
  • Recent Passport-Size Photo of Student
